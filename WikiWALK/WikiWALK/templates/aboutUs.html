ABOUT US
  • This game was created by a team of 6 sophomores studying at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad.
  • It is essentially our project for the course Algorithm Analysis and Design.
  • The inspiration behind this game was the idea of six degrees of separation which signifies that all people on average are six, or fewer, social connections away from each other.
  • Our primary focus was to apply this idea to wikipedia articles and depict that it does not only pertain to humans.
  • We hope you enjoy playing this game and can appreciate the main objective of it
