HOW TO PLAY
  • After clicking on the play button, you will be directed to the game page which depicts a starting point and ending point from one article to another.
  • The primary goal is to traverse from one article to another in the shortest possible path.
  • After choosing an article from the list provided, that article becomes the current article and newly linked articles are provided in the list.
  • To aid the user, a peek option is provided which reveals the Wikipedia article for any option in the list.
  • The user can continue to select articles until they are notified that they have reached the end point.
  • We Hope You Enjoy!!
